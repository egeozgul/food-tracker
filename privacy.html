Privacy Policy — Food Tracker

Last updated: July 2026

Food Tracker is a personal, single-user application built and used by its owner to log daily food intake, protein, and calorie data, and to optionally sync calorie-burn data from a connected Whoop account.

Data collected (food entries, calorie/protein goals, and Whoop calorie-burn figures) is stored privately in a Firebase database accessible only to the authenticated account owner. Whoop account tokens are stored securely on a private backend and are never exposed to any client, third party, or public interface.

This application does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. It does not serve ads or use analytics beyond basic application functionality.

Access to Whoop data can be revoked at any time by disconnecting Whoop within the app, or by revoking access directly from your Whoop account settings.

Questions about this application can be directed to the contact listed in its Whoop Developer Platform app registration.
